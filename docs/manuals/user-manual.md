# StreamVerse User Manual

## Introduction
Welcome to StreamVerse, your premium streaming platform. This manual guides you through the features and functionalities of the StreamVerse application.

## Getting Started

### Account Creation
1. Navigate to the **Sign Up** page.
2. Enter your email, password, and preferred username.
3. Select your subscription tier (Basic, Standard, Premium).
4. Complete the payment process.

### Logging In
1. Enter your registered email and password on the **Login** page.
2. Click **Sign In**.

## Features

### Browsing Content
- **Home**: Discover trending, popular, and recommended content tailored just for you.
- **Search**: Use the search bar to find movies, TV shows, and live streams by title, genre, or cast.
- **Categories**: Browse content by genres like Action, Comedy, Drama, and more.

### Playback
- Click on any title to start watching.
- Use the player controls to play/pause, adjust volume, toggle subtitles, and change video quality (up to 4K).
- **Resume Watching**: Continue where you left off from the "Continue Watching" section on the Home screen.

### Personalization
- **Watchlist**: Add titles to your watchlist to save them for later.
- **Ratings**: Rate content to improve your recommendations.
- **Profiles**: Create multiple profiles for family members to keep recommendations separate.

### Account Management
- **Profile Settings**: Update your email, password, and profile picture.
- **Subscription**: Manage your subscription plan and payment methods.
- **Devices**: View and manage devices logged into your account.

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues
- **Buffering**: Check your internet connection. Try lowering the video quality.
- **Login Failed**: Ensure your credentials are correct. Use the "Forgot Password" link if needed.
- **Playback Error**: Refresh the page or restart the app.

### Support
For further assistance, contact our support team at support@streamverse.com or visit our Help Center.
