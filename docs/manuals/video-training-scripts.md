## Script 2: Handling Customer Support Tickets (For Support Agents)
**Duration**: 3 Minutes
**Visual**: Agent Dashboard login screen.

**Audio (Narrator)**: "As a StreamVerse Support Agent, your dashboard is your command center. Let's walk through handling a ticket."

**Visual**: Dashboard showing a list of "Open" tickets. Cursor selects one.
**Audio**: "Start by selecting an open ticket from the queue. Prioritize 'High' severity issues."

**Visual**: Ticket details view. Agent typing a response.
**Audio**: "Review the user's issue carefully. Use the Knowledge Base sidebar to find quick solutions. Always respond with empathy and clarity."

**Visual**: Agent clicking "Resolve" and then "Close Ticket".
**Audio**: "Once the issue is fixed, mark the ticket as resolved. Ensure the user is satisfied before closing."

**Visual**: "Thank You" screen.
**Audio**: "Efficient support keeps our users happy. detailed guides are available in the Training Manual."

## Script 3: Content Moderation Guidelines (For Moderators)
**Duration**: 2.5 Minutes
**Visual**: CMS Interface showing "Pending Review" queue.

**Audio (Narrator)**: "Content safety is our priority. Here is how to review user-generated content."

**Visual**: Moderator clicking on a reported comment.
**Audio**: "When a comment is flagged, review the context. Does it violate our Community Guidelines?"

**Visual**: Highlighting "Hate Speech" and "Spam" options in the rejection menu.
**Audio**: "If it does, select the appropriate reason for removal. This helps train our automated filters."

**Visual**: "Approve" button being clicked for a safe comment.
**Audio**: "If the content is safe, simply click Approve to clear the flag."

**Visual**: StreamVerse Trust & Safety Logo.
**Audio**: "Thank you for keeping StreamVerse safe for everyone."
