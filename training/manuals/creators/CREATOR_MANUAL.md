## Getting Started as a Creator

### Becoming a Creator

1. Sign in to your StreamVerse account
2. Go to **[studio.streamverse.io](https://studio.streamverse.io)**
3. Click **"Become a Creator"**
4. Complete creator profile:
   - Channel name
   - Description
   - Profile picture
   - Banner image
   - Social media links
5. Agree to Creator Terms of Service
6. Submit for review (24-48 hours)

- ‚úÖ Valid StreamVerse account

## Uploading Content

### Upload Process

1. Click **"Upload Video"** button
2. **Select File**: Drag & drop or browse
3. **Add Details**:
   - Title (max 100 characters)
   - Description (max 5,000 characters)
   - Thumbnail (1920x1080, JPG/PNG)
   - Category/Genre
   - Tags (for discovery)
   - Language
   - Maturity Rating
4. **Advanced Settings**:
   - Release date (schedule)
   - Visibility (Public, Unlisted, Private)
   - Comments (enable/disable)
   - Age restrictions
5. **Upload & Process**

### Supported Formats

**Video:**
- **Formats**: MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WebM
- **Codecs**: H.264, H.265/HEVC, VP9
- **Resolution**: Up to 8K (7680x4320)
- **Frame Rate**: 24, 30, 60 fps
- **Bitrate**: Variable (recommended 8-50 Mbps for 1080p)

**Audio:**
- **Formats**: AAC, MP3
- **Channels**: Stereo, 5.1 surround
- **Sample Rate**: 48 kHz
- **Bitrate**: 192-320 kbps

### File Size Limits

| Plan | Max File Size | Max Duration |
|------|---------------|--------------|
| Basic | 5 GB | 60 minutes |
| Pro | 20 GB | 180 minutes |
| Enterprise | 100 GB | Unlimited |

### Transcoding

StreamVerse automatically creates:
- Multiple quality versions (SD, HD, 4K)
- HLS/DASH streaming formats
- Thumbnails and preview clips
- Auto-generated subtitles (AI)

Processing time: 5-30 minutes (depending on duration/quality)

## Monetization

### Monetization Requirements

To enable monetization:
- ‚úÖ 1,000+ subscribers
- ‚úÖ 4,000+ watch hours (last 12 months)
- ‚úÖ Adherence to Content Guidelines
- ‚úÖ Verified payment information
- ‚úÖ Tax information submitted

### Revenue Streams

**1. Ad Revenue**
- Pre-roll, mid-roll, post-roll ads
- Display ads
- Revenue share: 55% to creator, 45% to platform

**2. Subscriptions**
- Channel subscriptions ($4.99-$24.99/month)
- Exclusive content for subscribers
- Revenue share: 70% to creator

**3. Pay-Per-View**
- Set price per title ($0.99-$19.99)
- Perfect for premium content
- Revenue share: 70% to creator

**4. Tips & Donations**
- Viewers can tip during live streams
- One-time or recurring donations
- 100% to creator (minus payment processing)

### Setting Up Monetization

1. **Apply for Monetization**: Creator Studio ‚Üí Monetization
2. **Review & Accept Terms**: Partner Program Agreement
3. **Set Up Payment**: Add bank account or PayPal
4. **Submit Tax Info**: W-9 (US) or W-8BEN (International)
5. **Enable on Content**: Choose monetization per video

### Payment Schedule

- **Threshold**: $100 minimum
- **Payment Dates**: Monthly (1st week of month)
- **Method**: Bank transfer or PayPal
- **Reporting**: Detailed revenue reports

## Analytics & Insights

### Overview Metrics

- **Views**: Total and unique views
- **Watch Time**: Total hours watched
- **Average View Duration**: Engagement metric
- **Impressions**: Times thumbnail shown
- **Click-Through Rate (CTR)**: Thumbnail effectiveness
- **Subscribers Gained/Lost**: Growth trends

### Audience Demographics

- **Age**: Viewer age distribution
- **Gender**: Audience breakdown
- **Geography**: Top countries/cities
- **Devices**: Mobile, web, TV split
- **Traffic Sources**: How viewers found content

### Engagement Metrics

- **Likes/Dislikes**: Viewer sentiment
- **Comments**: Engagement level
- **Shares**: Social sharing
- **Add to Watchlist**: Interest indicator
- **Completion Rate**: % who watched to end

### Real-Time Analytics

Monitor active viewers:
- Current viewers
- Peak concurrent viewers
- Viewer location
- Traffic sources (live)

### Revenue Analytics

- **Estimated Revenue**: Projected earnings
- **Revenue by Source**: Ad, subscription, PPV breakdown
- **RPM (Revenue Per Mille)**: Revenue per 1,000 views
- **Playback-Based CPM**: Ad revenue per 1,000 plays

## Best Practices

### Content Quality

**Video Quality:**
- Film in at least 1080p (4K preferred)
- Use good lighting
- Stable camera work (tripod/gimbal)
- Clear audio (external mic recommended)

**Thumbnails:**
- Eye-catching imagery
- Readable text (mobile-friendly)
- Consistent branding
- 1920x1080 resolution
- Faces perform well

**Titles:**
- Clear and descriptive
- Include keywords
- Create curiosity
- Avoid clickbait
- 60 characters or less

**Descriptions:**
- First 2 lines show in preview
- Include keywords naturally
- Add timestamps
- Link to related content
- Call-to-action

### SEO & Discoverability

1. **Keywords**: Research popular search terms
2. **Tags**: Use 10-15 relevant tags
3. **Categories**: Choose accurate category
4. **Subtitles**: Improve searchability
5. **Engagement**: Encourage likes, comments, shares

### Audience Engagement

- **Respond to Comments**: Build community
- **Ask Questions**: Encourage discussion
- **Create Polls**: Interactive content
- **Host Live Streams**: Real-time engagement
- **Behind-the-Scenes**: Connect with audience

### Upload Schedule

- **Consistency**: Upload regularly
- **Frequency**: Quality over quantity
- **Timing**: Upload when audience is active
- **Series**: Release episodes on schedule

### Content Strategy

1. **Know Your Audience**: Understand viewer preferences
2. **Analyze Performance**: Learn from analytics
3. **Experiment**: Try new formats
4. **Collaborate**: Work with other creators
5. **Evolve**: Adapt to trends and feedback
