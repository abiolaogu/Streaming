# Ad Compositing Service

This service is responsible for the real-time, AI-powered compositing of advertisements directly into video streams. It is a core component of the StreamVerse monetization strategy, replacing traditional ad insertion methods.

## Key Responsibilities

-   Analyzes video streams to identify suitable surfaces and objects for ad placement.
-   Selects and retrieves relevant ad creatives based on video context and user data.
-   Composites ad creatives into the video stream in real-time.
