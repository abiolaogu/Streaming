# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Introduction

This document outlines the product requirements for the StreamVerse platform, a next-generation streaming service designed to support 100 million subscribers. The platform will provide a seamless and engaging user experience, with a focus on scalability, reliability, and performance.

## 2. Product Vision

To become the leading global streaming platform, offering a diverse range of content to a massive and engaged audience. The platform will be known for its high-quality user experience, personalized content recommendations, and robust and scalable infrastructure.

## 3. Target Audience

The platform will target a global audience of all ages, with a focus on users who are passionate about streaming content and looking for a high-quality and personalized experience.

## 4. Key Features

*   **Scalable Streaming**: The platform will be able to handle millions of concurrent users, with a focus on low latency and high-quality video playback.
*   **Personalized Recommendations**: The platform will use machine learning to provide personalized content recommendations to each user.
*   **Multi-Device Support**: The platform will be available on a wide range of devices, including web, mobile, and smart TVs.
*   **Creator Studio**: The platform will provide a suite of tools for content creators to upload, manage, and monetize their content.
*   **Social Features**: The platform will include social features that allow users to interact with each other and share their favorite content.

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements

*   **Scalability**: The platform must be able to support 100 million subscribers and handle millions of concurrent users.
*   **Reliability**: The platform must be highly reliable, with a goal of 99.999% uptime.
*   **Performance**: The platform must be highly performant, with low latency and fast loading times.
*   **Security**: The platform must be highly secure, with robust protection against all types of attacks.
*   **Maintainability**: The platform must be easy to maintain and update, with a focus on clean code and good documentation.
